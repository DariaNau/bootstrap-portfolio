About Me

Full Stack Web Developer leveraging business management skills to ensure efficiency in mobile-first design and innovative web development.

My education background consists of Bachelor’s degree in Business Management from South Ural State University in Russia, Master’s degree in Professional Marketing Communication from Clark University in Massachusetts, and a certificate in Full Stack Development from Georgia Tech University with hands-on experience in JavaScript, Responsive Web Design, Node.js as well as C#.

Over five years of international experience in hospitality management has prepared me for open and clear communication to benefit teamwork and promote customer-focused perspectives on user’s interaction with websites and software platforms. Additionally, two years in social media assistant role has equipped me with a solid understanding of content management and marketing initiatives proven by received Rising Star Award recognizing significant company’s growth influenced by an excellent social media strategy.

With my passion for programming challenges coupled with a commitment to ensuring business success I am positioned well to contribute to the growth of a fast-paced, quality-driven tech team in order to build better experiences on the web.
